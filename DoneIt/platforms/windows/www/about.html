About Me

Hi! I'm DoneIt, a simple task tracking app with group functionality.

My designers created me with one simple idea in mind, to create a user-friendly app that people could use to assist them, their family, and friends in managing their busy lives.


Terms to know

A "DoneIt" is a task.

A "Group" is anyone with which you would like to share and complete tasks.
